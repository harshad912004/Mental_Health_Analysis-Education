Common Problems in the Education System with Solutions:

1. Career Anxiety:

  • Problem: Many students face uncertainty about career paths, leading to high stress and anxiety.
  • Solution: Promote access to Counseling Access. Career workshops targeted at courses/years with highest average anxiety levels.

2. Poor Sleep Hygiene:

  • Problem: Excessive Social Media Usage hours â†’ Reduced Sleep Hours, impacting mental health and academic focus.
  • Solution: Implement digital detox campaigns in hostels and colleges. Promote time management training.

3. Low Counseling Access:

  • Problem: Low awareness or usage of mental health counseling.
  • Solution: Mandatory orientation about counseling services. Embed mental health programs into curriculam.

4. Overloaded Exam Schedules:

  • Problem: High Exam Frequency â†’ Higher Career Anxiety Level.
  • Solution: Review academic policies to balance exams vs continuous assessment. Include open-book or applied learning methods.

5. Disengagement in Campus Life:

  • Problem: Students without Club_Participation may show lower CGPA or higher loneliness.
  • Solution: Actively encourage club participation via incentives or credits. Create more inclusive clubs (not just sports/cultural).

6. Attendance Issues:

  • Problem: Low Attendance_% in certain groups indicates disengagement.
  • Solution: Early intervention programs for students with low attendance. Gamify attendance or provide flexible learning modules.

7. Living Condition Stressors:

  • Problem: Hostel students or day scholars may experience different stress triggers.
  • Solution: Design living condition-specific support (hostel mentorship, day scholar groups). Offer flexible counseling slots (online/offline).
